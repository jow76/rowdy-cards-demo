Example Custom Rules:

- Max's Rule

When a player finishes their drink, they can pick someone to drink.

- J-Hon's Rule (The Yoinker)

If someone has to drink more than 1, anyone can choose to steal half their drinks (rounded down)

- Jow's Rule

When anyone has to drink, 1 other person can activate this rule. The current player now has to drink double the amount but the player who called the rule must also drink the original amount.

- Mattbear Rule (Hibernate)

You can choose to hibernate your drinks but, every 2 rounds, increase the amount by 1. You can drink the hibernated drinks whenever but if the game ends you must drink all hibernated drinks.

- Banker's Rule

When you have to drink, you can drink 3 to activate this rule. Add your turns drinks to a bank, then continue round all players, adding their drinks to the bank, until it reaches you again. Whoever had to drink the highest amount on their turn drinks the entire banked amount.
